Joe, a character who exists in both New York and the world before birth, tried to keep the essence of the real world intact. "The size and shape are different, but the soul 'Joe' and the person 'Joe' have a visual connection," said animation director Jude Brownville. The proportion of the features and the size of the trademark fedora, glasses and gestures are different, but they are designed to look the same to the audience.
